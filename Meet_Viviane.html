Viviane Girardin

Intern Candidate

Viviane holds a Master's degree in environmental toxicology from the University of Oslo with focus on contaminants in the aquatic environment. Always in contact with the ocean, Viviane became the youngest diving instructor certified in Brazil at the minimum age of 18. As an instructor and underwater photographer/videographer she explored different oceans for many years before settling in Oslo in 2008. She has a vast knowledge various electronic tools such as PowerPoint, Prezi and Photoshop. She has especial interest in programming languages including R, Python and markup language HTML. During her period working at the Brazilian-Norwegian Chamber of Commerce, Viviane re-structured and re-designed the organization's website entirely. Currently she is a teaching assistant for the project Carpentry@UiO, a volunteer effort that offers workshops in foundational coding and data science skills to graduates and researchers.
